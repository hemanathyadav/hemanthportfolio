Top 3 Telugu Movies in 2026

1. Dacoit: A Love Story (2026)

A raw and intense love story starring Adivi Sesh and Mrunal Thakur, this film is acclaimed for its gripping narrative and intense action sequences. It follows an angry convict seeking vengeance against his betrayer, providing a high-octane cinematic experience.

2. Peddi (Expected June 2026)

Directed by Buchi Babu Sana and starring Ram Charan, this film is set in the 1980s Andhra Pradesh, focusing on a, spirited villager uniting his community, promising a blend of emotional depth and strong character-driven drama.

3. Goodachari 2 / G2 (Expected May 1, 2026)

Starring Adivi Sesh, this high-budget spy thriller continues the journey of Agent Gopi, promising global-scale action, intricate twists, and a patriotic tone that expands on its predecessor's success.
